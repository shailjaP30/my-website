Places I Have Livd In

Kathmandu,Nepal

This is where I was born and spent the first 14 years of my life. Kathmandu, often called the gateway to tourism in Nepal, is the heart of the country’s economy and culture. Boasting the most advanced infrastructure in Nepal, this vibrant city is a blend of rich history, breathtaking landscapes, and lively traditions—a must-visit destination for every traveler.

Siliguri,India

I completed my higher education and lived here for four years. Known as the "Gateway to Northeast India," Siliguri is famous for its three Ts—tea, timber, and tourism. Nestled at the foothills of the Himalayas, it is beautifully situated on the banks of the Mahananda and Teesta Rivers.

Delhi,India

I’m currently living in Delhi, India, a vibrant city known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and dynamic energy. I’m pursuing my graduation in Software Engineering from Delhi Technological University, where I’m honing my skills and preparing for an exciting future in technology.
